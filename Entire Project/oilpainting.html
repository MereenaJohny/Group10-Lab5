Oil Paintings

MainPage

Oil paint is a combination of a pigment and an oil binding medium. It dries slowly in comparison to other paints, allowing the artist to manipulate the paint. The binder is usually linseed oil. The methods used to process the oil affect the colour, consistency and drying time of the paint.
